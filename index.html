CALLIGRAPHY

SIX MONTHS DIPLOMA

This course will guide you, step-by-step, through the tools to use, and the scripts to produce to give you a professional expertise in this beautiful art form.

The aim of this course is to promote and revive this art which is an integral part of our Islamic Art heritage.

The course is suitable for the beginners as it will start from the very basics and move towards advance level under the guidance of professional and experienced calligrapher.

Instructor

Kamal Hassan is a professional calligrapher and has more than 10 years of experience in this field...

FAST FACTS

Duration:
6 Month
Eligibility:
Matriculate or equivalent
Class Timings:
2:00 pm to 5:00 pm (Mon & Wed)
Qualification:
Diploma Certificate

FEES

Admission Fee:
PKR 12,000
Tuition Fee:
- Full-Course (lump sum): PKR 54,000
- Monthly Fee: PKR 7,000

COURSE CONTENT

History of calligraphy

Materials of traditional calligraphy

Crafting the bamboo qalam and cut pen

Traditional (aahar) paper making

Ink making

Making a traditional calligraphy art piece

Composition-making in all these scripts
