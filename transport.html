Transport

The Institute of Qalamkar provides safe and reliable transport services for its students. Our fleet of vehicles covers major areas to ensure easy access to the institute. Each vehicle is well-maintained and follows all safety and cleanliness standards.

We have trained and responsible drivers who prioritize student safety. Transport schedules are designed to match class timings for convenience. Special transport arrangements are available for workshops and calligraphy events. Parents are kept informed of routes and timing through regular communication.

GPS tracking and attendance systems ensure student security during travel. Affordable transport fees make it accessible for all students. Our goal is to provide a stress-free, comfortable journey every day.

Note:
  • These transport services are contractual and not owned by Qalamkarschoolandcollege.
  • The administration only monitors the transporters’ performance and timing.
  • All payment and fee-related matters should be handled directly with the transporters.
