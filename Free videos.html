Modern Script

Modern script is a free-flowing and fluid calligraphy style. It often combines cursive with a more casual look, making it popular for personal correspondence.

Fonts: Brush Script, Allura

Italic

Italic calligraphy features flowing, slanted letters. It's a versatile style often used for invitations, letter writing, and greeting cards.
