Library of Qalamkar school&College

Our Calligraphy Institute Library is a dedicated space for creativity and learning. It houses a rich collection of books on calligraphy, typography, art, and design. Students can explore classical scripts and modern styles through our curated resources. We provide access to rare manuscripts and digital archives for deeper research.

The library supports both beginners and advanced learners in their artistic journey. Comfortable reading areas and a quiet environment enhance focus and inspiration. Workshops and study sessions are regularly hosted within the library space.

Our goal is to preserve the tradition of calligraphy while encouraging innovation. Experienced librarians are available to assist with research and resource finding. This library is a treasure trove for every calligraphy enthusiast and artist
